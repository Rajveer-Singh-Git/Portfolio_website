SERVICES
I design and develop fully responsive websites that adapt seamlessly to desktops, tablets, and smartphones. Using fluid grids, flexible images, and CSS media queries, I ensure your site looks perfect on every screen—no more pinching or zooming.
I refine your site’s usability with intuitive layouts, readable typography, and interactive elements designed for both desktop and mobile. From button sizes to scroll behavior, every detail is optimized for engagement.
A slow site drives visitors away. I boost your speed by compressing images, minifying code, and implementing lazy loading—so your site loads in under 3 seconds (and ranks higher on Google).
I write clean, semantic HTML that search engines adore. Your site will be mobile-friendly (a Google ranking factor!), with structured data and optimized metadata to attract more organic traffic.
Is your site stuck in 2010? I’ll transform it into a modern, responsive masterpiece with improved accessibility (WCAG compliance), faster performance, and a design that wows on any device.
I rigorously test your site on real devices (iPhones, Androids, tablets) and browsers (Chrome, Safari, Firefox) to squash bugs and ensure pixel-perfect consistency everywhere.
Responsive design isn’t a one-time job. I offer monthly updates, troubleshooting, and fixes to keep your site running smoothly as new devices and browsers emerge.
